# about

Arina is a Senior Business Developer at Workforce Integration Solutions (WIS), where she focuses on transforming talent matching through verified credentials and skills-based hiring. She is also the London Co-Lead for Future of Higher Ed (FOHE) and a Venture Scout at Ada Ventures.

Previously, she founded [Let Me Graduate](https://letmegraduate.softr.app/), a non-profit initiative that improved access to higher education by highlighting alternative pathways to degrees.

Arina earned her Bachelor of Science degree from Excelsior College. Her professional interests stem from her personal experience navigating the U.S. higher education system. Arina's degree is a combination of learning opportunities spanning over a dozen providers earned between the ages of 14 and 25 - representing both the flexibility and complexity of navigating the pathway to a degree.
